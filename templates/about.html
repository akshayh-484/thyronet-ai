🏥 About ThyroNet: Thyroid Nodule Analysis

Our Mission

To provide accurate, fast, and accessible thyroid cancer detection using advanced artificial intelligence, helping healthcare professionals make informed decisions and improve patient outcomes.

🤖 What is ThyroNet: Thyroid Nodule Analysis?

ThyroNet: Thyroid Nodule Analysis is an advanced medical diagnostic system that uses artificial intelligence to detect thyroid cancer through two complementary approaches:

🖼️ Image Analysis

Hybrid deep learning ensemble combining ResNet50, ResNeXt50, and DenseNet121 with weighted soft voting (45%, 45%, 10%) achieves 99% accuracy on thyroid ultrasound images.

📊 Clinical Analysis

Machine learning model (CatBoost) evaluates 53 clinical features including demographics, medical history, and lab results with 82% accuracy.
