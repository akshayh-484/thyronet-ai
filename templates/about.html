About ThyroNet

AI-powered thyroid cancer analysis — combining deep learning image analysis with clinical ensemble models.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accurate, fast, and accessible thyroid cancer detection using advanced artificial intelligence, helping healthcare professionals make informed decisions and improve patient outcomes.

Image Analysis

A 3-stage deep learning pipeline: three YOLO models detect and localize the nodule region, then an 8-model classifier ensemble (DenseNet121, ResNet50, ResNet152, MobileNetV3, EfficientNetB4, VGG16, InceptionV3, ConvNeXt) classifies the crop.

87% ensemble accuracy · TN5000 dataset

Clinical Analysis

Multiple ensemble ML models evaluate clinical features — thyroid disease prediction (9,168 patients, 93.4% accuracy) and cancer risk scoring (50,000 samples, 85% accuracy).

93.4% peak accuracy · disease prediction
